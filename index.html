This site is a passion project, created as a reference for all of the mechanics and key abilities to be found across the group content in The Elder Scrolls Online. It is designed to be used more as a reference in order to devise a strategy for dealing with a particular mechanic yourself or your group are struggling with. A hidden "spoiler" section will be included at the bottom of each section detailing one approach to dealing with that particular stage (likely one of many) but I'd advise you to work with the group you have and adjust as needed or, better yet, ignore this section altogether and come up with your own strategy as a group.

All ability names are taken directly from ESO log files and so you should be able to get a clear description for any mechanic that may be giving you difficulty by simply navigating to the correct section and performing a search.
